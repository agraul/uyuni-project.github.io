IRC

Our official #uyuni channel is hosted at Freenode. Join with your favourite IRC client, or use the web application.

Currently we do not required a registered nickname to join, but you will required it to talk because of recent spambot attacks to Freenode.

Mailing lists

  • uyuni-announce: To receive news about the Uyuni (moderated)

  • uyuni-devel: To discuss about Uyuni development (anyone can post as long as it is registered)
